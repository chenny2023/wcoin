Tekel Data — The Transparent Data Layer for iGaming

iGaming has one defining problem: opacity, fraud and noise. Operators self-report their numbers, "volume" is inflated by wash trading, and most "reviews" are paid placements. Tekel Data is the opposite by design — a radically transparent, public data layer where nothing is taken on trust. We map operators' on-chain wallets, read their all-chain proof-of-reserves, measure real deposit/withdrawal flow (stripping the wash and treasury churn that inflates headline figures), and blend that with multi-source trust ratings. Every figure is measured, sourced, and something you can re-check yourself on a public block explorer.

The name is the point. Tekel — "weighed in the balance." We put each operator's real, measured data on the scale against the hollow claims, so you can weigh it yourself before you deposit. We run no casino, take no affiliate placement for rankings, and never label an operator "safe" or "scam" — we surface verifiable signals and let you decide. Coverage spans 11+ blockchains, on-chain prediction markets and gambling-streamer analytics. All data is public and free — no login, no paywall.
