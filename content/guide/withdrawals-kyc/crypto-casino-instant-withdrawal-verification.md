## Why "Instant Withdrawal" Claims Often Fail
Many crypto casinos advertise instant withdrawals, but when users actually try to cash out, they face delays, freezes, or sudden KYC hurdles. The root cause is usually a liquidity problem: the platform lacks the reserves to pay players and relies on a flow of new deposits to process older withdrawals. If on-chain reserves drop, the casino may freeze withdrawals to prevent a bank run.
