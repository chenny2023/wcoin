A sign up bonus looks attractive on the landing page, but it means nothing if the casino is insolvent. The safest welcome offers come from operators with verifiable on-chain liquidity. ## How do fake volume and wash trading affect your bonus?

Many crypto casinos inflate their transaction volume to look popular and solvent. They do this by cycling funds between internal hot wallets, double-counting deposits, or churning treasury funds. A casino might show millions in daily "volume" and use that apparent success to market a generous sign up bonus. But if that volume is fake, the platform may not have the real liquidity to honor your withdrawal.
